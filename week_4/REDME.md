# PMAY-G Performance & Social Convergence Dashboard 🏠📊

## 📌 Project Overview
This **Capstone Project** was developed during my Power BI Internship at **MS ELEVATE** (Microsoft & AICTE initiative). The project transforms national-level data from the **Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana - Gramin (PMAY-G)** into a high-level Business Intelligence suite to monitor housing targets, administrative efficiency, and social impact.

## 📂 Repository Structure
Based on the project architecture, the repository is organized as follows:
*   📁 **Dataset/**: Contains the raw and processed data used for analysis.
*   📁 **Report/**: The primary `.pbix` Power BI project file.
*   📁 **Screenshots/**: High-resolution images of the 3-page dashboard and Data Model.
*   📄 **PMAY-G Performance & Social Convergence.pptx**: Presentation detailing the project methodology and key findings.
*   🖼️ **logo.jpg**: Project branding and assets.

## 🚀 Key Analytical Pillars

### 1. Physical Progress & Implementation
* **Scope:** Analyzed **2.97 Cr sanctioned houses** and **1.94 Cr completions**.
* **Insights:** Identified peak completion trends in FY 2022 and benchmarked top-performing states like Bihar.

### 2. Efficiency Audit (Diagnostic Analytics)
* **Scope:** Analyzed the **400-day average delivery lifecycle**.
* **Insights:** Deconstructed the timeline into **Admin Time**, **Construction Time**, and **Transaction Time** to identify process bottlenecks.

### 3. Social Convergence (Impact Mapping)
* **Scope:** Monitored **85%+ saturation** in basic beneficiary amenities.
* **Insights:** Visualized the "Service Delivery Funnel" tracking active **Ujjwala (LPG)** connections and **SBM (Sanitation)** seeding.

## 🛠️ Technical Stack & Skills
* **Tool:** Power BI Desktop
* **Data Modeling:** Architected a **Star Schema** with multi-fact tables and shared Dimension tables.
* **ETL:** Advanced **Power Query** for cleaning and merging diverse government datasets.
* **DAX:** Developed complex measures for Performance Indexing, YoY growth, and Lead-Time calculations.
