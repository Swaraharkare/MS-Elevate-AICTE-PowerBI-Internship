# PMAY-G Performance & Social Convergence Dashboard 🏠📊

## 📌 Project Overview
This **Capstone Project** was developed during my Power BI Internship at **MS ELEVATE** (a Microsoft & AICTE initiative). The project transforms national-level data from the **Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana - Gramin (PMAY-G)** into a high-level Business Intelligence suite to monitor housing targets, administrative efficiency, and social impact.

## 🚀 Key Analytical Pillars

### 1. Physical Progress & Implementation
* **Scope:** Analyzed **2.97 Cr sanctioned houses** and **1.94 Cr completions**.
* **Insights:** Identified peak completion trends in FY 2022 and benchmarked top-performing states like Bihar.
* **Visuals:** Achievement vs. Target gauges and time-series trend lines for state-wise performance.

### 2. Efficiency Audit (Diagnostic Analytics)
* **Scope:** Analyzed the **400-day average delivery lifecycle**.
* **Insights:** Deconstructed the timeline into **Admin Time**, **Construction Time**, and **Transaction Time** to identify process bottlenecks.
* **Impact:** Correlated administrative lead-times with project completion delays to drive process optimization.

### 3. Social Convergence (Impact Mapping)
* **Scope:** Monitored **85%+ saturation** in basic beneficiary amenities.
* **Insights:** Visualized the "Service Delivery Funnel" from 3.9M registrations to 3.2M active **Ujjwala (LPG)** connections and **SBM (Sanitation)** seeding.

## 🛠️ Technical Stack & Skills
* **Tool:** Power BI Desktop
* **Data Modeling:** Architected a **Star Schema** with multi-fact tables (Physical, Financial, Social) and shared Dimension tables (State, Year).
* **ETL:** Advanced **Power Query** for data cleaning, pivoting, and merging diverse government datasets.
* **DAX:** Developed complex measures for Performance Indexing, YoY growth, and Lead-Time calculations.
* **UX/UI:** Custom navigation pane, dynamic slicers, and conditional formatting for executive-level readability.
